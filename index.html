How Close Is Quantum Computing to Being Useful?

A plain-English map of where quantum computing actually stands and an honest estimate of its pacing. "Useful" runs on three clocks: narrow scientific value now through 2030; broad commercial value in the 2030s; and a code-breaking machine that experts now put at roughly one-in-three odds within ten years — which is why banks, governments, and Bitcoin are already migrating off today's encryption.
